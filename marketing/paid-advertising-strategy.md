# Paid Advertising Strategy

Organic growth (SEO, social media) builds long-term equity, but Paid Advertising (PPC) is required for immediate lead generation. Our strategy focuses on capturing high-intent searchers (Google) and driving awareness (Meta).

## 1. Google Ads (Search & Performance Max)
Google Ads is our highest priority paid channel because users searching here have **high commercial intent**—they need cash *now*.

### Campaign Structure
*   **Campaign 1: Branded Search**
    *   **Keywords:** "Coin Care Capital", "Coin Care Logbook Loans"
    *   **Goal:** Defend our brand name from competitors bidding on it. Keep CPC (Cost Per Click) very low.
*   **Campaign 2: High-Intent Non-Brand**
    *   **Keywords:** [Exact Match & Phrase Match] "logbook loans Nairobi", "logbook loans in Kenya", "cash against logbook", "car title loan Kenya", "loans without CRB check".
    *   **Goal:** Capture active searchers.
*   **Campaign 3: Competitor Targeting**
    *   **Keywords:** Bidding on names of major Kenyan logbook lenders (Mogo, Platinum Credit, Momentum).
    *   **Ad Copy Angle:** "Faster approvals than [Competitor]? Try Coin Care. Cash in 24 hrs."
*   **Campaign 4: Performance Max (PMax)**
    *   **Goal:** Let Google's AI find conversions across YouTube, Display, Search, and Gmail using our best images and ad copy.

### Optimization Tactics
*   **Negative Keywords:** Exclude terms like "unsecured loans", "salary advance", "fuliza", "tala", "branch" to avoid paying for clicks from users who don't own cars.
*   **Ad Extensions:** Use Sitelink extensions (About Us, Calculator), Call extensions (Click-to-call direct to our sales team), and Location extensions.

## 2. Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)
Meta ads excel at generating demand by putting our offer in front of vehicle owners who might not be actively searching but need capital.

### Campaign Structure
*   **Campaign 1: Lead Generation (In-App Forms)**
    *   **Goal:** Lowest friction possible. Users fill out their name, phone, and car make directly inside Facebook/IG without visiting the website.
    *   **Audience:** Broad targeting (Age 25-55, Location: Nairobi + major towns), allowing Meta's algorithm to find the buyers.
*   **Campaign 2: Website Conversions (Traffic to Landing Pages)**
    *   **Goal:** Drive users to the Coin Care website `/calculator` or home page to submit the Next.js Apply form.
    *   **Audience:** Interest targeting (Vehicle Owners, SME Business Owners, Entrepreneurship).
*   **Campaign 3: Retargeting (The "Follow-Up")**
    *   **Goal:** Show ads to people who visited our website but didn't apply.
    *   **Audience:** Website visitors (last 30 days) tracked via the Meta Pixel.
    *   **Ad Copy Angle:** "Still thinking about it? Use our calculator to see how much cash you can unlock from your car today."

### Creative Strategy
*   **Video performs best:** Short, UGC (User Generated Content) style videos of a person explaining the process standing next to a car.
*   **Before/After states:** "Stressed about stock clearance? -> Happy business owner with funded inventory."
*   **Clear text overlays:** "Up to 60% of vehicle value", "No CRB checks", "Approval in 24hrs".

## 3. Budget Allocation & Tracking
*   **Initial Split:** 60% Google Ads (capturing existing demand), 40% Meta Ads (creating demand and retargeting).
*   **Attribution:** Ensure Google Tag Manager is installed on the Next.js site to track form submissions as conversions. Pass this data back to Google and Meta to train their algorithms.
*   **KPIs:** 
    *   Cost Per Lead (CPL)
    *   Lead-to-Funded-Loan Conversion Rate
    *   Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
