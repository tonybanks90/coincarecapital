# SEO & Content Strategy

To ensure Coin Care Capital ranks high on Google for relevant search terms in Kenya (e.g., "logbook loans Nairobi", "cash against logbook", "fast loans Kenya"), we must adhere to a strict SEO strategy.

## 1. Technical SEO Configuration (Next.js)

The current web application utilizes Next.js App Router, which provides excellent SSR (Server-Side Rendering).

*   **Metadata API:** Each page uses the `export const metadata` object.
*   **Dynamic Routes:** The blog uses dynamic routes (`/blog/[slug]`) to generate unique pages for every article.
*   **Semantic HTML:** We use `<h1>` (only once per page), `<h2>`, `<h3>`, and `<section>` tags to structure content logically.
*   **Performance:** Image optimization via `next/image` ensures fast LCP (Largest Contentful Paint) scores.

## 2. Keyword Strategy

Our primary keyword clusters are:
1.  **Transactional:** `logbook loans`, `cash against car`, `car title loans`.
2.  **Informational:** `how to get a loan with a logbook`, `CRB clearance for loans`, `vehicle valuation Nairobi`.
3.  **Local:** `logbook loans Nairobi`, `logbook lenders in Kenya`.

## 3. Blog Content Pillars
The blog (`/blog`) is the primary driver for informational keywords.
*   **Pillar 1:** Education & Financial Literacy (e.g., "Top 5 Mistakes to Avoid When Taking a Loan").
*   **Pillar 2:** Business Growth (e.g., "Why You Should Use Your Car to Fund Your Business").
*   **Pillar 3:** Product Explanations (e.g., "How Do Logbook Loans Work in Kenya?").
