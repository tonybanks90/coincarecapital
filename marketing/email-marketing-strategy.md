# Email & SMS Marketing Strategy

Email and SMS marketing represent the highest ROI channels for Coin Care Capital. While email is excellent for B2B clients and detailed newsletters, **SMS is critical** for the Kenyan market where open rates exceed 95%.

## 1. Automated SMS Workflows

SMS is our primary transactional and urgent communication channel. We will integrate a local gateway (e.g., Africa's Talking) for these automated flows:

### The "Application Received" Flow
*   **Trigger:** User submits the "Apply Now" form.
*   **Message (Immediate):** "Hello [Name], we've received your application for vehicle [Reg Number]. A Coin Care loan officer will call you within 15 mins for your free valuation. Need help now? WhatsApp us at wa.me/254720078432"

### The "Incomplete Application" Flow (Drop-off Recovery)
*   **Trigger:** User enters their phone number on Step 1 of the modal but closes it before Step 3.
*   **Message (After 30 mins):** "Hi [Name], you started a logbook loan application with Coin Care but didn't finish. Reply 'YES' if you need a callback from our team to assist you, or click [Link] to continue."

### The "Repayment Reminder" Flow
*   **Trigger:** Upcoming monthly payment.
*   **Message (3 days before):** "Dear [Name], your Coin Care loan installment of KES [Amount] is due on [Date]. Pay via Paybill [Number], Account [Reg Number]. Keep your account in good standing!"
*   **Message (Day of):** "Friendly reminder: Your logbook loan installment of KES [Amount] is due today. Please ensure payment to Paybill [Number] to avoid late fees."

## 2. Automated Email Workflows

Email is used for lead nurturing, particularly for business owners (B2B) who may take longer to decide compared to emergency individual borrowers.

### The B2B Welcome Nurture Sequence
*   **Trigger:** User downloads a "Business Financing Guide" or signs up for a newsletter on the B2B page.
*   **Email 1 (Immediate):** Welcome & The Guide Delivery. "Here is your guide to scaling with asset finance."
*   **Email 2 (Day 3):** Case Study. "How [Company Name] expanded their delivery fleet using their existing logbooks."
*   **Email 3 (Day 7):** Trust & Authority. "Why 500+ Kenyan SMEs trust Coin Care over traditional banks (No 6-month wait, no rigid CRB checks)."
*   **Email 4 (Day 10):** Direct Pitch. "Ready to unlock the capital in your fleet? Reply to this email to schedule a free consultation with our B2B finance director."

### The "Post-Loan" Lifecycle
*   **Trigger:** Loan is fully cleared and logbook is returned.
*   **Email 1 (Day 1):** Thank you & Feedback Request. "Congratulations on clearing your loan! Please leave us a Google Review [Link]."
*   **Email 2 (Month 3):** Re-engagement. "Need capital again? As a returning customer with excellent history, you are pre-approved for up to KES [Amount] at preferred rates. Click here to claim."

## 3. The Monthly Newsletter ("The Coin Care Ledger")

Sent to all active and past clients, as well as prospects who opted in.
*   **Purpose:** Remain top-of-mind so when they face a cash crunch, they think of Coin Care.
*   **Content:** 
    *   1x Financial tip (e.g., "How to prepare your business for the KRA end-of-year rush").
    *   1x Vehicle maintenance tip (since the car is the collateral, keeping it in good shape benefits both parties).
    *   1x Company update (new branch, new product offering).
*   **Frequency:** Once a month (mid-month, before payday crunches).

## Compliance & Privacy
*   **Opt-out:** All SMS messages must include "Stop*456*X#" (or standard local opt-out). All emails must have a clear "Unsubscribe" footer.
*   **Data Protection:** Adhere to the Kenyan Data Protection Act by ensuring numbers and emails are only used for the stated purposes.
