# Marketing Strategy Hub

Welcome to the Coin Care Capital marketing strategy documentation. This section outlines the comprehensive multi-channel approach we will use to acquire, engage, and retain customers for our logbook loan products.

Our marketing efforts are divided into four core pillars:

1. **[Search Engine Optimization (SEO)](./seo-strategy.md)**
   Capturing high-intent users actively searching for "logbook loans" and "fast cash" in Kenya.

2. **[Social Media Strategy](./social-media-strategy.md)**
   Building brand trust, sharing testimonials, and educating our audience across Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and X (formerly Twitter).

3. **[Paid Advertising Strategy](./paid-advertising-strategy.md)**
   Accelerating lead generation through targeted Google Ads and Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Ads.

4. **[Email & SMS Marketing](./email-marketing-strategy.md)**
   Nurturing incomplete applications, sending payment reminders, and driving repeat business through automated communication flows.

## Marketing Goals

*   **Primary Metric:** Number of qualified loan applications submitted via the website.
*   **Secondary Metric:** Cost Per Acquisition (CPA) of a funded loan.
*   **Tertiary Metric:** Brand awareness and social engagement rate.

## Target Audience Persona

*   **Demographics:** 25-55 years old, primarily based in Nairobi Metro and major Kenyan towns.
*   **Business Owners (SMEs):** Need quick working capital to clear goods, buy stock, or expand without giving up equity or dealing with slow bank approvals.
*   **Employed Professionals:** Need emergency cash for medical bills, school fees, or unforeseen expenses.
*   **Pain Points:** Traditional banks take too long, demand too much paperwork, and have strict CRB checks.
*   **Value Proposition:** Fast approval (under 24 hours), keep driving your car, flexible repayment terms, minimal paperwork.
