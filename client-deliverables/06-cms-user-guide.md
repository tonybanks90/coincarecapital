## What is the CMS?

The **CMS (Content Management System)** is the admin panel where you can update website content — like blog posts, FAQs, and site settings — **without touching any code** or asking the developer.

Think of it as a "back office" for your website.

---

## How to Access the CMS

1. Open your web browser (Chrome recommended)
2. Go to: **`https://[your-domain]/admin`**
3. Log in with your Sanity account (the developer will create your account)
4. You'll see the Sanity Studio dashboard

> [!TIP]
> Bookmark `https://[your-domain]/admin` for quick access.

## What You Can Edit

### 1. Site Settings (Global)

These settings affect the **entire website** — not just one page.

**How to edit:**
1. In the left sidebar, click **"Site Settings"**
2. Click on the single document that appears
3. Edit any field:

| Field | What it controls | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Brand Name** | Company name displayed on the site | "Coin Care Capital" |
| **Tagline** | The subtitle on the home page hero | "Get up to 60% of your vehicle's value in cash within 24 hours." |
| **Phone Number** | Displayed in footer and contact page | "0720 078 432" |
| **Email Address** | Displayed in footer and contact page | "coincarecapital@gmail.com" |
| **Address** | Physical address in footer | "P.O BOX 26993 - 00100, G.P.O Nairobi" |
| **WhatsApp URL** | All WhatsApp buttons on the site | "https://wa.me/254720078432" |
| **Loan Interest Rate** | Used in the loan calculator | "3.5%" |

4. Click **"Publish"** (green button, bottom right)
5. Changes appear on the live website within **30 seconds**

---

### 2. Blog Posts

**How to create a new blog post:**
1. In the left sidebar, click **"Blog Post"**
2. Click the **"+"** button (top right) to create a new post
3. Fill in the fields:

| Field | Required? | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Title** | ✅ Yes | The headline of your article |
| **Slug** | ✅ Yes | Click "Generate" — this creates the URL (e.g., `logbook-loans-kenya`) |
| **Author** | No | Defaults to "Coin Care Team" |
| **Main Image** | No (but recommended) | Upload a banner image (minimum 1200px wide) |
| **Category** | No | e.g., "Tips", "News", "Guides" |
| **Published At** | No | Set the publish date (for ordering) |
| **Excerpt** | No | A short 1–2 sentence summary (shown on the blog listing page) |
| **Content** | No | The full article text |
| **Read Time** | No | e.g., "5 min read" |

4. Click **"Publish"**
5. Your post appears on the `/blog` page within 30 seconds

**How to edit an existing post:**
1. Click **"Blog Post"** in the sidebar
2. Click on the post you want to edit
3. Make your changes
4. Click **"Publish"**

**How to delete a post:**
1. Open the post
2. Click the **"..."** menu (top right)
3. Select **"Delete"**
4. Confirm deletion

---

### 3. FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

FAQs appear on the **home page** in the accordion section.

**How to add a new FAQ:**
1. Click **"FAQ"** in the left sidebar
2. Click **"+"** to create a new FAQ
3. Fill in:
   - **Question:** The question customers ask (e.g., "What documents do I need?")
   - **Answer:** Your detailed answer
4. Click **"Publish"**

**How to edit or delete:**
- Same process as blog posts — click the FAQ, edit, and publish.

## Important Tips

### ✅ Do's
- **Do** click "Publish" after every change — drafts are NOT visible on the website
- **Do** use the "Generate" button for slugs instead of typing them manually
- **Do** upload high-quality images (minimum 1200px wide)
- **Do** write clear, concise FAQ answers

### ❌ Don'ts
- **Don't** delete the Site Settings document — there should only be one
- **Don't** worry about breaking the website — content changes only affect text and images
- **Don't** use all-caps in titles (the design handles text styling)
- **Don't** upload very large images (over 5MB) — they slow down the site

## Updating the WhatsApp Number

If your WhatsApp number changes:
1. Go to **Site Settings**
2. Update the **WhatsApp URL** field
3. Use this format: `https://wa.me/254XXXXXXXXX` (country code + number, no spaces, no dashes)
4. Publish

All WhatsApp buttons across the entire website will update instantly.
