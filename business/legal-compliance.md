# Legal & Compliance Framework

Operating a credit-only microfinance or logbook lending business in Kenya requires strict adherence to various legal frameworks.

## 1. Central Bank of Kenya (CBK) Regulation

*   **Digital Credit Providers (DCP) Regulations 2022:** All digital lenders operating in Kenya must be licensed by the CBK. Coin Care Capital must display its CBK license status prominently on the website footer and About page to build trust.
*   **Anti-Money Laundering (AML) & KYC:** We must adhere to the Proceeds of Crime and Anti-Money Laundering Act (POCAMLA).
    *   *Action:* Valid National ID, KRA PIN, and facial verification (via passport photo or physical meeting) are mandatory for all loans.

## 2. NTSA & Vehicle Ownership

*   **Joint Registration:** Coin Care does not take physical possession of the car. Instead, a joint registration is placed on the logbook via the NTSA TIMS / eCitizen portal. 
*   *Action:* The logbook reads: `Coin Care Capital / [Customer Name]`.
*   **Caveats:** For smaller loans or short-term bridging finance, a legal caveat can be placed on the logbook instead of full joint registration to prevent the owner from transferring the vehicle.

## 3. Data Protection Act (2019)

As a lender, we collect highly sensitive data. The Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) mandates:
*   **Registration:** Coin Care must be registered as a Data Controller with the ODPC.
*   **Consent:** Explicit consent must be collected via the web form before processing PII.
*   **Data Breach Notification:** We are legally obligated to report any database breaches to the ODPC within 72 hours.

## 4. Consumer Protection

*   **Transparency:** The Consumer Protection Act requires that all terms, including the Total Cost of Credit (APR + Fees), are clearly stated in the loan offer letter.
*   **Debt Collection:** Harassment of borrowers or their contacts (a common issue with rogue mobile loan apps) is strictly illegal and revokes the CBK DCP license. Repossession of the collateral vehicle must follow the Auctioneers Act.
