# 💰 Pricing, Fees & Repayment Structure

This document outlines the standard payment terms, interest rates, and fee structures for Coin Care Capital logbook loans. It is designed as an internal reference for loan officers to ensure transparency and consistency when calculating customer disbursements and repayments.

---

## 1. Interest Rates

Our goal is to remain highly competitive against major players like Platinum Credit and Mwananchi Credit, while maintaining healthy profit margins.

*   **Standard Rate:** **3.5% to 5% per month** (depending on the vehicle's age and the borrower's risk profile).
*   **Calculation Method:** **Reducing Balance**. 
    *   *Why?* Calculating interest on a reducing balance (rather than a flat rate) is much more attractive to borrowers. It means their monthly interest burden decreases as they pay off the principal, which is a major selling point our sales agents can use.
*   **Repayment Tenure:** Flexible options from **1 month to 24 months**.

---

## 2. Loan-To-Value (LTV) Limits

We lend against the **Forced Sale Value (FSV)** of the vehicle, not the current market value. This protects the company in case of default and auction.

| Vehicle Type | Max LTV (of Forced Sale Value) | Max Loan Tenure |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Personal Cars (Saloons/Estates) | Up to **70%** | 24 Months |
| Commercial Vehicles (Pickups/Lorries) | Up to **60%** | 18 Months |
| PSV (Matatus) | Up to **50%** | 12 Months |
| Tractors & Heavy Machinery | Up to **50%** | 12 Months |

> [!IMPORTANT]  
> Vehicles older than 12 years from the date of manufacture automatically have their maximum LTV reduced by 10%.

## 3. Upfront Fees & Deductions

To ensure the borrower doesn't have to pay out of pocket before receiving the loan, all fees are **deducted upfront from the approved loan amount** before disbursement to their M-Pesa or bank account.

### A. Processing Fee
*   **Amount:** **3%** of the total approved loan amount.
*   **Purpose:** Covers administrative overhead, credit checking, and internal loan processing.

### B. Valuation Fee
*   **Amount:** **KES 3,000 - KES 4,500** (depending on vehicle location and type).
*   **Purpose:** Paid to our empaneled third-party valuers to accurately determine the Forced Sale Value.

### C. NTSA Joint Registration Fee
*   **Amount:** **KES 1,500 to KES 3,000** (varies by engine capacity/CC).
*   **Purpose:** Covers the TIMS/eCitizen portal fees for registering a caveat and transferring the logbook to joint ownership (Coin Care Capital & the Borrower).

### D. Tracking System Installation
*   **Amount:** **KES 4,000 - KES 6,000**
*   **Purpose:** Installation of a GPS tracker. 
*   *Note:* If the client already has an active, approved tracker that we can ping, this fee is waived.

### E. Comprehensive/Credit Life Insurance (If Applicable)
*   **Amount:** Usually **4% to 5%** of vehicle value per annum.
*   **Purpose:** If the borrower does not have comprehensive insurance, we arrange premium financing and deduct the first premium from the loan.

## 4. Repayment Breakdown Example

**Scenario:** A customer is approved for a **KES 500,000** logbook loan, to be repaid over **12 months** at **4% per month (reducing balance)**.

### Disbursement Math:
1.  **Approved Loan:** KES 500,000
2.  **Minus Processing Fee (3%):** - KES 15,000
3.  **Minus Valuation:** - KES 3,500
4.  **Minus NTSA Caveat:** - KES 2,500
5.  **Minus Tracker Installation:** - KES 5,000
6.  **Net Amount Sent to Customer:** **KES 474,000**

### Repayment Schedule:
*   The customer will pay a fixed monthly installment (Principal + Interest) calculated via an amortization schedule.
*   Payments are made directly to our Paybill number, referencing their unique Loan ID.

---

## 5. Penalties & Default Protocol

Strict enforcement of penalties is required to encourage timely payments.

*   **Late Payment Penalty:** **5% flat fee** applied to the overdue installment amount if unpaid 3 days after the due date.
*   **Bouncing Cheque Fee:** **KES 2,500** per bounced cheque (if post-dated cheques are used).
*   **Repossession Trigger:** If a loan is in arrears for **30 days** with no communication or restructuring agreement, the vehicle is flagged for repossession. A repossession fee (standard market rate, ~KES 15,000 - 30,000) will be added to the customer's outstanding balance.
